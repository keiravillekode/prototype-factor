# Learning

- [Factor tutorial](https://andreaferretti.github.io/factor-tutorial/) by Andrea Ferretti -- a comprehensive introduction covering the stack, combinators, vocabularies, and more.
- [Learn Factor in Y Minutes](https://learnxinyminutes.com/factor/) -- a quick tour of Factor syntax and features.
- [Official documentation](https://docs.factorcode.org/) -- the complete reference for all built-in vocabularies and words.
- [Your first program](https://docs.factorcode.org/content/article-first-program.html) -- the official walkthrough for writing, testing, and documenting a vocabulary.
- [Factor blog](https://factor-language.blogspot.com/) -- articles and announcements from the Factor developers.
- [Concatenative wiki: Factor](https://concatenative.org/wiki/view/Factor) -- community wiki with tutorials, examples, and FAQ.
